CORE QUESTION

In a system with no villains,
how does inequality happen —
and who is responsible?

This is the hinge of the talk. Everything before was setup; everything after is exploration.
Q1

If you were an investor in Apple, would you push them to share more profits with supply chain workers?

YES   ·   NO

Real discussion — not rhetorical. Let people who say No explain themselves first.

Who bears the most responsibility for this inequality?

No clean answer. Surface what assumptions the audience holds — and why those intuitions might be incomplete.

If you joined Apple's Finance Team
and your manager asked you to cut costs —
would you have the power, or the willingness, to ask:
Who is paying the price for this cut?

This isn't a moral question. It's a career question.
